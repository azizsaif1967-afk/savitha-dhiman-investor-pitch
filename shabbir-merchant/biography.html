The Biography

From a Dh 1,500 salary to the UAE's signage standard

Early life & heritage

Born in December 1958 in Karachi, Pakistan, Shabbir Merchant grew up in a family already anchored in the signage trade. His late father, M Y "Yousuf" Merchant, migrated from Bombay to Karachi in 1947 and became a pioneer of outdoor advertising and neon signs in Pakistan — first through a Lahore partnership, then building a wider reputation in hand-blown glass neon.

That inheritance of craft, discipline and a love of light-up letters would later shape the Champion story in Dubai.

Arrival in the UAE

In 1976, at just 18, Shabbir Merchant arrived in the UAE at the invitation of his elder brother Hanif Merchant — founder of what is today Emirates Neon Group. His first role was alongside Hanif, learning the fundamentals of the UAE signage market: how to price, source, install, and keep a customer for life.

His starting salary was Dh 1,500 a month. The habit he built around that paycheck — saving, re-investing, refusing shortcuts — became the financial backbone of the company he would one day start on his own.

"Customers are the backbone of any business. You can build with better machines, faster software or cheaper material — but nothing replaces a client who trusts you enough to call you back." — Shabbir Merchant, Gulf News Success Stories

The Company He Built

Champion Group

Champion Group has grown from a one-product neon workshop into an integrated signage, digital and outdoor-media manufacturer — widely recognised as one of the UAE's most trusted visual-communication brands. Core divisions include:

Neon · Since 1989

Champion Neon

Hand-blown glass neon, LED-flex neon, illuminated 3D letters and heritage signage. The founding division of the group.

LED & Interactive

Champion Digital

Fixed and rental LED screens, LCD displays, wayfinding, interactive kiosks. Led by Shazil Merchant.

Print & Branding

Champion Prints

Vehicle wraps, window graphics, exhibition stands, corporate branding — end-to-end design & fabrication.

Outdoor Media

Champion Outdoor

Billboards, building wraps, pylon signs and large-format displays across all seven emirates.

Facility & reach

The Champion Experience Centre and production campus occupies 65,000 square feet in Dubai Investments Park II, with branch offices in Sharjah, Ajman, Abu Dhabi, Fujairah and Ras Al Khaimah. Over 150 employees represent Pakistan, India, Nepal, Bangladesh, the Philippines and Africa.

Clients & sectors

Champion serves clients across automotive, retail, food & beverage, hospitality, construction, corporates and banks. Public reporting cites 1,000+ clients served, 100,000+ signs delivered, and annual turnover in the Dh 25–30 million range.
