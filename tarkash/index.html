About Tarkash FZE

Empowering Workforces.
Elevating Businesses.
Building a Responsible Future.

Tarkash FZE is a trusted workforce solutions partner based in Sharjah, UAE, delivering ethical, reliable, and future-ready labour recruitment and staffing services across the region. For years we have supported organisations with Labour Recruitment Brokerage, On-Demand Labour Supply and Permanent Recruitment Solutions that are fast, compliant, and built on integrity.

Our story is shaped by the vision of our Founder, Dr. Syed Aftab Ahmed, who has lived and worked in the UAE for over 30 years. With senior leadership experience in global organisations and more than 13 years successfully running HR & Management Consulting firms in UAE, he brings deep regional insight, strong governance values and a people-first philosophy to every service we deliver.

Frequently Asked

Clear answers. No fine print.

Which industries does Tarkash FZE serve?+
We proudly serve clients across construction, logistics, hospitality, manufacturing, retail, facility management, healthcare and energy — anywhere workforce quality and compliance matter.
How quickly can you mobilise labour?+
For on-demand supply, our trained, documented talent pool allows us to mobilise workers within 24–72 hours depending on scale, role and location.
Do you handle UAE visas and documentation?+
Yes. Our brokerage service covers transparent documentation, visa compliance and onboarding aligned with UAE labour regulations — so you carry no hidden risk.
What guarantees do you offer on permanent hires?+
All permanent placements are backed by a structured replacement guarantee and our retention-focused assessment process, which prioritises long-term cultural fit over speed alone.
Can you scale for bulk hiring and project peaks?+
Absolutely. We regularly mobilise 50+ workers for short-term project peaks across construction, logistics and facility management, with dedicated deployment coordinators.
How do you integrate ESG and DEI into recruitment?+
Through inclusive sourcing strategies, bias-free assessments, fair-pay audits, pay-equity reviews and ESG-linked leadership scorecards — practical, measurable, board-ready.
