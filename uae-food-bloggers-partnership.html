Partnership Strategy: How to Approach Food Bloggers for EatCookJoy.com Launch

1. Tiered Outreach Framework

Mega Influencers (1M+) - Brand Ambassadors

Budget: AED 15,000-50,000 per post
Approach: Formal email via management/agent. Propose exclusive "Founding Ambassador" role with equity/revenue share.
Ask: 1 launch video + 3 story mentions + profile link for 3 months
ROI: Mass awareness, credibility, press coverage
Targets: Bader Al Safar, Hussain Sallam, Mr Taster, Moayad AlSawaf

Macro Influencers (100K-999K) - Launch Partners

Budget: AED 3,000-15,000 per post
Approach: DM + email. Offer paid partnership + exclusive early access to app + "Featured Reviewer" badge.
Ask: 1 reel/TikTok reviewing a chef booked through the app + story swipe-up
ROI: Targeted reach in UAE foodie community
Targets: JustFoodDXB, FoodFindsUAE, Arezoo, Reza Kiani, Haya Kaabar

Mid-Tier (10K-100K) - Content Army

Budget: AED 500-3,000 per post OR barter (free chef experience)
Approach: Instagram DM with personalized message. Offer free chef booking experience + content creation kit.
Ask: Try a chef through the app, post honest review (reel + stories)
ROI: High engagement, authentic content, community trust
Best for: Bulk outreach - aim for 30-40 mid-tier partnerships

Micro/Nano (<10K) - Grassroots Squad

Budget: Free chef booking (AED 200-500 value) or affiliate commission
Approach: Friendly DM, feel personal. Offer affiliate code with 10-15% commission per booking.
Ask: Post about experience, share affiliate code with followers
ROI: Highest engagement rates (5-10%), hyper-local trust, cost-effective
Best for: Sustained long-term growth after launch

3. Campaign Phases

Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4 weeks before)

- Sign 2-3 mega influencers as ambassadors
- Onboard 10 macro influencers for launch day content
- Build waitlist using influencer-shared signup links
- Teaser content: "Something is cooking in Dubai..."

Phase 2: Launch Week

- Coordinated post drop: All partners post within 48 hours
- Host exclusive launch dinner with top 20 bloggers + chefs
- Live cooking session with mega influencer on Instagram
- PR push: "Dubai's first chef-on-demand app"

Phase 3: Growth (Months 1-3)

- Weekly "Chef of the Week" features with mid-tier bloggers
- Activate 50+ micro influencers with affiliate codes
- Monthly cooking competitions judged by top bloggers
- UGC campaign: #EatCookJoy customer reviews

Phase 4: Scale (Months 3-6)

- Expand to Abu Dhabi & Sharjah with local bloggers
- Launch "EatCookJoy Certified Reviewer" program
- Seasonal campaigns: Ramadan, Eid, National Day
- Cross-promote with fitness/wellness influencers

- 85% of hospitality brands report increased footfall after influencer visits

Best Posting Times for UAE

- Instagram: Thu-Fri 12pm-2pm, 7pm-9pm GST
