About the Shop

A sharp cut shouldn't be complicated.

Tucked into Muwaileh Commercial on Sheikh Khalifa Bin Zayed Al Nahyan Street, Elegance Limit Gents Saloon is the kind of barbershop the neighbourhood keeps coming back to. We're not a luxury spa or a high-street chain — we're a working men's saloon that takes pride in doing the basics beautifully.

Walk in for a classic fade, a straight-razor beard shape, a hot-towel shave, or a facial that leaves your skin feeling new. Our six chairs are sanitised after every sitting, our tools are clean, and our barbers know how to listen before they cut.
