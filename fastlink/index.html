FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is FastLink free for candidates?

Yes — 100% free. We are paid by the employer. You'll never receive an invoice from us.

How long does it take to get matched to a role?

First shortlist within 24 hours of CV submission. Most candidates have an interview scheduled within 5 business days.

Do you sponsor visas?

Yes — we handle UAE, KSA, Qatar and 90+ other countries. Most placements include full visa, medical, and family status where applicable.

What types of roles do you recruit for?

From C-suite executives and engineers to skilled trades, hospitality, healthcare, and general manpower. If it's a job, we likely cover it.

Will my CV be shared without permission?

Never. We always check before sending your profile to a client and brief you fully on the role before any introduction.

What if I'm currently outside the UAE?

No problem — 60% of our placements are international candidates. We coordinate visas, flights, and onboarding end-to-end.

How quickly can FastLink shortlist candidates?

3–5 vetted CVs land in your inbox within 24 hours of brief. Specialist roles may take 48 hours; bulk mobilisation is scheduled by week.

What is your replacement guarantee?

3-month free replacement on every permanent hire. If a candidate leaves or doesn't pass probation, we replace them at no cost.

How does your EOR pricing work?

From $299/employee/month + a one-time setup. No hidden fees — full breakdown on the AI quote tool above.

Can you handle bulk mobilisation (50+ hires)?

Yes — our largest single mobilisation was 480 site staff for a UAE megaproject in 8 weeks. We have dedicated mobilisation desks for India, Philippines, Egypt and Nepal.

What's the difference between EOR and Manpower Supply?

EOR = we are the legal employer, you direct the work (used for white-collar). Manpower Supply = same model but priced per-day for blue-collar/skilled trades.

Are you MOHRE-licensed?

Yes — fully licensed by the UAE Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation, plus equivalent licenses in KSA, Qatar, India and Philippines.

Can you support payroll-only (we found the candidate)?

Absolutely — payroll-only outsourcing starts at $79/employee/month and includes WPS/GOSI compliance, payslips, and full reporting.
