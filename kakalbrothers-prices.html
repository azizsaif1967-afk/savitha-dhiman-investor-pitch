Our Story
Four generations of bespoke brass.

Since 1960, Kakal Brothers has been crafting India's finest luxury hardware — brass handles and knobs encrusted with Swarovski elements. Based in Mumbai, we have supplied discerning homes, hotels and palaces across India and beyond.

Our master artisans work exclusively with pure brass and S.S. 304 stainless steel — no compromises on quality. The majority of our production is in pure brass and stainless steel, delivering products of superior design whether traditional, contemporary, or avant-garde.

Setting the standard for premium hardware craftsmanship in India since 1960.

The Art of Making
From raw brass to polished perfection.

Four stages. Countless hands. One standard — uncompromising.

Design

Each piece begins with a sketch. Our designers blend traditional aesthetics with contemporary sensibility, drafting every curve before a tool is lifted.

Material Selection

We source only pure brass, S.S. 304 stainless steel, and genuine Swarovski crystal elements. No alternates. No substitutes.

Precision Crafting

Master artisans shape each handle with meticulous attention — every joint, every curve meets our exacting standards before it leaves the bench.

Finishing & Polish

A multi-stage plating and polish process gives every finish its lasting depth — from warm antique to mirror chrome.
