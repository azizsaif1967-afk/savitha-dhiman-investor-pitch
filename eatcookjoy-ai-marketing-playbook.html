# EatCookJoy UAE — Product Marketing Context

## What we are
EatCookJoy UAE is a marketplace + managed service that sends vetted
personal chefs to homes, villas, and offices across all 7 UAE emirates
for weekly meal prep, dinner parties, and corporate catering.

Tagline: "Your Personal Chef, Right at Your Door."
Website: eatcookjoy-uae.com

## Core offerings & price anchors (AED, inclusive)
- Weekly meal prep (bulk cook-session)       from AED 450
- Light meal prep (busy-professional plan)   from AED 300
- Dinner party (3-course, up to 8 guests)    from AED 600
- Event / corporate catering                 POA (custom quotes)

## The promise
- 500+ curated recipes, every allergen tracked & labelled
- 50+ vetted chefs across Arabic, Levantine, Indian, Italian, Pan-Asian, clean-keto
- Google Calendar integration for booking & reminders
- 4.9★ from 1,200+ reviews
- Coverage: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, RAK, Fujairah, UAQ

## Target audience (the 4 personas)
1. "Busy Pro Priya" — 32, Dubai Marina, management consultant, wants
   healthy lunches delivered so she stops ordering Talabat at 10pm.
2. "Family Fatima" — 38, Arabian Ranches, 2 kids, one dairy-free, wants
   Sunday-through-Wednesday dinner sorted without supermarket stress.
3. "Host Hassan" — 45, Emirates Hills, entertains business partners
   monthly, wants Michelin-style dinners without hiring full-time staff.
4. "HR Hana" — 29, corporate HR at DIFC firm, books Thursday team lunches
   + Ramadan iftar for 40-person office.

## Brand voice
- Warm, generous, hospitable — Arab + Mediterranean hospitality.
- Confident but never boastful. Quietly premium.
- Bilingual-ready: English primary, Arabic phrases welcomed ("bil hana
  wa shifa," "sahtein"), never forced.
- No stock-photo adjectives. Never say "delicious," "mouth-watering,"
  "exquisite." Instead describe the dish specifically.
- Tone by channel:
  - Instagram captions: playful, sensory, 1–2 emojis max
  - WhatsApp: direct, one-thing-per-message, no essays
  - Landing pages: confident, specific numbers, testimonials above fold
  - Email: personal-sounding, from the chef or founder, never "Dear Valued Customer"

## Competitors (UAE)
- Kitopi — cloud kitchens, scale, lower price, less personal
- Munch:on — corporate subscriptions, B2B heavy
- Calo — meal-plan subscription, fitness-coded, no live chef
- Chef for a Night — private-chef marketplace, similar model, smaller chef pool
- Careem Food / Talabat — delivery apps, not meal-prep

## Differentiators (lead with these)
- Real chef in your kitchen, not a dark-kitchen reheat
- 7-emirate coverage (most rivals are Dubai-only)
- Full allergen tracking on every recipe
- Transparent chef profiles (name, face, cuisine, years of experience)
- One-click re-order of a chef you loved

## Key differentiators NOT to claim
- We are not cheapest. Do not compete on price.
- We are not a subscription box. Every week is fresh & custom.
- We are not a restaurant. Do not use restaurant adjectives.

## Current priorities (this quarter)
- Grow Abu Dhabi bookings (30% of bookings vs 55% in Dubai)
- Launch Ramadan iftar-at-home package (families of 4–10)
- Build corporate-catering pipeline in DIFC / ADGM
- Reduce CAC on Meta ads (currently AED 180, target AED 110)

## Compliance
- All chefs: Dubai Municipality food handler certificate
- All recipes: allergen labels required (UAE FoodWatch standard)
- Never claim medical benefits ("cures," "heals," "treats")
- Alcohol: offer BYOB only, never serve or supply

# EatCookJoy UAE — AI Marketing Team

## Team overview
You manage a 5-agent marketing team for EatCookJoy UAE, a personal-chef
& meal-prep service covering all 7 emirates.

Always read `.agents/product-marketing-context.md` before routing ANY task.

## The agents
- souq-analyst       — data, analytics, SEO, A/B
- food-writer        — copy, captions, emails, WhatsApp
- majlis-scout       — competitor & market research
- plate-stylist      — visual briefs, shot lists, decks
- season-strategist  — campaigns, launches, orchestration

## Routing rules
- "analyse," "how are bookings," "CAC," "SEO"     → souq-analyst
- "write," "caption," "email," "bio," "copy"      → food-writer
- "competitor," "Kitopi," "trend," "research"     → majlis-scout
- "brief," "carousel," "photo," "deck," "visual"  → plate-stylist
- "campaign," "launch," "Ramadan," "summer"       → season-strategist

## Default collaboration for campaigns
When the task is a full campaign (Ramadan / summer / new offering):
1. majlis-scout gathers the market scan
2. season-strategist writes the brief
3. food-writer drafts copy (EN + AR tone)
4. plate-stylist produces the visual brief
5. souq-analyst defines tracking & success metrics

## Output standards (every agent, every time)
- Start with: **Goal** (one sentence)
- End with: **Next action** (who does what by when)
- Always name the persona being addressed
- Always quote a specific dish, chef, or emirate — never generic
- Never use: "delicious," "mouth-watering," "exquisite"
- AED prices, never $ or €

## Local rules
- Use UAE spelling & English (realise not realize)
- Prayer times & Ramadan hours matter — adjust send times
- Avoid alcohol in hero imagery unless the brief explicitly says so
- Summer (Jul–Aug) = freezer-pack angle, not dinner-party angle

## Notion task board
Before starting any session, check the Notion board "ECJ Marketing" for
"To Do" cards. Pick up the oldest, move to "In Progress," complete,
move to "Done" with a 3-line summary attached.

Step-by-step

  1. Install Node.js (nodejs.org) then VS Code (code.visualstudio.com).
  2. Install the Claude Code extension in VS Code · sign in with Anthropic.
  3. Create folder eatcookjoy-marketing/ and open it in VS Code.
  4. Open the terminal (Ctrl + ~) and run:
    npx skills add coreyhaines31/marketingskills
  5. Create .agents/product-marketing-context.md — paste Section 02 content.
  6. Create the 5 agent folders and paste each AGENT.md from Section 03.
  7. Create root CLAUDE.md — paste Section 05 content.
  8. (Optional) Set up Notion board "ECJ Marketing" and install Nano Banana MCP for remote task pickup.
  9. Run your first task: in the Claude Code terminal, type: "Rewrite the homepage hero for EatCookJoy UAE with 3 variants aimed at Busy Pro Priya"
  10. Claude auto-routes to food-writer + copywriting + page-cro. Review, tweak, ship.
Week 1 win condition: hero rewrite live, Kitopi comparison page drafted, first 10 Instagram captions queued. If you hit that, the system is working.
